Awarded for the originality of the idea, which created an external compiler on a server with secure access on Moscow HSE Hackathon featuring FCS

On September 17-18, 2022, the Moscow HSE Hack Hackathon was held, organized by the FCS Hackathon Club. The hackathon was aimed at developing solutions for import substitution. His idea is not to copy what already exists, but to come up with a unique feature that will distinguish the solution from similar ones, and create a prototype. Emphasis was placed on open source solutions, since they are not tied to a specific country. The PiedPiper team was also awarded for the originality of the idea, which created an external compiler on a server with secure access. Participants: Jan Miller, 2nd year Cybersecurity MIEM, Alexey Krokhin, 2nd year Cybersecurity MIEM; Maksim Krutonog, 2nd year student at Cybersecurity MIEM.
